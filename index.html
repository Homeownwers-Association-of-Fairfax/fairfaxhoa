Community description

A place to call home

The Fairfax neighborhood is a well-established residential community featuring 248 single-family homes nestled among mature trees and landscaped common areas. Founded in 1987, Fairfax has grown into a close-knit community known for its pride of ownership, friendly neighbors, and exceptional amenities.

Our neighborhood offers a quiet, family-friendly atmosphere with convenient access to top-rated schools, shopping, dining, and major commuter routes — all while maintaining the character of a true community.

Neighborhood Amenities

  • Community swimming pool with pool house
  • Two lighted tennis courts
  • Scenic walking paths and green spaces
  • Children's playground and picnic area
  • Professionally maintained landscaping
  • Holiday lighting and seasonal decorations

How we're governed

About the HOA

The Fairfax Homeowners Association is a nonprofit organization that manages and maintains the community's common areas, enforces community standards, and fosters neighborly relationships throughout the neighborhood.

The HOA is governed by a five-member Board of Directors elected annually by homeowners. The board meets monthly and welcomes resident participation and input.

Annual dues

  • Annual HOA assessment: $850 paid by check or $860 paid with Zelle per household
  • Due annually on January 1st
  • Covers maintenance, insurance, and amenities

Annual Meeting

  • June 6, 2027 at 6:00PM
  • Fairfax Community Center, Room B
  • Open to all residents — all are welcome

Community Amenities

Community Pool

Enjoy the Fairfax community pool, open each summer from Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day. The pool features a main pool and a pool house with restrooms and changing facilities.

Pool Hours

  • 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Pool Rules

  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
  • No animals are allowed in the pool or pool enclosure, except service animals, which are allowed on the deck.
  • No glass containers in the pool area
  • No diving in the shallow end
  • Pool may close for weather or maintenance
  • All children three years old and younger, and any child not potty-trained, must wear snug-fitting plastic pants or a water-resistant swim diaper.

Seasonal Information

  • Season opens: Memorial Day Weekend
  • Season closes: Labor Day

Tennis Courts

Fairfax features two freshly resurfaced hard courts, available to all residents year-round during daylight hours. Courts are equipped with lighting for evening play until 10:00 PM. Reservations and the live schedule below help avoid double bookings.

Court Rules

  • Residents only (guests permitted with resident present)
  • Proper tennis attire and non-marking shoes required
  • Maximum 90-minute play if others are waiting
  • No food or glass on the courts
  • Pets not permitted on court surfaces
